Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Quick Summary: We access only the transaction data you authorize. We use it to train AI models for YOUR use only. We don't share your data with third parties. You can revoke access anytime.

1. Introduction

CoKeeper ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our transaction categorization service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • CSV Files: Transaction data you upload for categorization
  • OAuth Authorization: Permission to access your QuickBooks or Xero data

2.2 Information We Collect via OAuth

When you connect your QuickBooks or Xero account, we access:

Data Type What We Access How We Use It
Transactions Date, vendor, amount, description, category Train ML models and generate predictions
Chart of Accounts Account names and codes Map predictions to your categories
Company Info Company name (QuickBooks realm ID) Session identification

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your QuickBooks or Xero passwords
  • ❌ Customer or vendor contact details
  • ❌ Bank account numbers or financial credentials
  • ❌ Personal identification documents

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • ML Model Training: Your historical transaction data trains custom models specific to YOUR business
  • Predictions: Trained models categorize new transactions
  • Session Management: OAuth tokens enable API access during your session

3.2 We Do NOT Use Your Data For

  • ❌ Training models for other users
  • ❌ Marketing or advertising
  • ❌ Selling to third parties
  • ❌ Credit scoring or financial analysis

4. Data Storage and Retention

4.1 What We Store

Data Type Storage Duration Location
OAuth Tokens Session duration (until revoked) Server file system
Trained ML Models Until you disconnect/delete Server file system
CSV Files Not stored (processed in memory) N/A
Transaction Data Not stored (processed in memory) N/A

4.2 Data Deletion

Your data is automatically deleted when you:

  • Revoke OAuth access from QuickBooks/Xero settings
  • Close your browser session
  • Request deletion (contact us)

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS: All data transmitted via encrypted connections
  • OAuth 2.0: Industry-standard authentication (no passwords stored)
  • Token Encryption: OAuth tokens stored securely
  • Server Security: Regular security updates and monitoring

5.2 Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your QuickBooks/Xero credentials secure
  • Review OAuth permissions before authorizing
  • Revoke access when no longer needed
  • Use strong passwords for your accounting platforms
